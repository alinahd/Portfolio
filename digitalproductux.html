Digital Product UX

Problem Statement

Traditional digital stories feel flat and disconnected, limiting immersion and engagement. Kids often lose interest in reading because storybooks can feel static compared to interactive media.

How might we keep children engaged with storytelling so that reading feels fun and interactive?

Value Proposition

Stories don’t just belong on paper. With AR, they unfold around you — blending sound, visuals, and interactive environments into an adventure you can step inside.

This app is the immersive AR storybook app that allows kids and families to experience stories through interactive visuals, spatial audio, and environments that come to life in their own space.

Key Features

  • AR World Integration: Stories unfold in the child’s own room with 3D characters and environments.
  • Synced Audio-Visual Narration: Narration, sound effects, and visuals progress together.
  • Multi-Theme Library: From fairy tales to educational adventures, stories adapt to different moods and learning goals.
