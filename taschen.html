Overview

Eco Taschen was a women-led sustainable brand focused on eco-conscious, oxo-biodegradable packaging for everyday essentials such as sanitary bags, pamper bags, fridge/freezer bags, trash bags and garment bags.

As a Design Intern, I contributed to digital content and product packaging that reflected the brand's environmental values while engaging a wider audience through visual storytelling.

Community Engagement

TNS x Taschen

This collaboration between Eco Taschen and TNS (The New School), my high school in Lahore, Pakistan, started as an idea I proposed during my internship. I wanted to create something that combined community engagement with sustainable design, using my connection with TNS to bring together shared values of creativity, environmental responsibility and education.

The brand was looking for ways to increase brand awareness and to communicate its sustainability mission and so this partnership became a meaningful way to do that. The concept focused on a community clean-up drive that highlighted the brand's eco-friendly trash bags while also encouraging students and local residents to take part in hands-on environmental action.

Product Design

Packaging Designs

As a design intern at Eco Taschen, I contributed to the development of packaging concepts for upcoming product lines. I supported the design team in creating visuals that reflected the brand's commitment to sustainability and clean, functional design.

I helped refine graphic details to highlight the recyclable nature of the materials, focusing on clarity and function through minimal graphics, thoughtful type placement, and a balance between practicality and aesthetics. I also collaborated with the production team to ensure the final packaging felt cohesive and true to the brand's identity.
